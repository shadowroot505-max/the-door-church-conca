Our Story

The Door Christian Centre Church was founded in 1995 with a simple vision: to be a beacon of hope in our city. Over the years, we have grown from a small gathering in a living room to a thriving congregation, but our core mission remains the same.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to lead people to become fully devoted followers of Christ. Our vision is to see lives transformed, families strengthened, and our community impacted by the love of Jesus.
